About Our Brand

BareNbliss is a clean beauty brand from Seoul, South Korea. It was co-founded by Jina Kim, a famous korean makeup artist, in 2021 The B+N+B philosophy (bare essentials, no harm, and bliss moments) was created by the korean skin lab by combining the highly effective ingredients and joyful experience in our products. The brand firmly avoids more than 2,000 harmful ingredients and selects active ingredients that is vegan and cruelty free, bareNbliss keeps on breaking through beauty boundaries to bring the innovative joyful product for everyone.
